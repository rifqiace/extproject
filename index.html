Uncertainty Check

(The measurements are subjected to uncertainty. Uncertainty analysis aims at identifying and quantifying the existing errors and trying to estimate the total uncertainty of a measurement.

The measurement uncertainty can be computed using the standard deviation of the probability distribution over the measured values that are attributed to quantify (Joint Committee For Guides In, 2008). Relative assessment of uncertainty can also be compared using the mean, median and mode.)
